01 — The Problem

Managing device repairs across multiple vendors is a hidden operational burden.

No accountability

When a device comes back from a local shop, there is no record of what was done, what parts were used, or whether they were genuine. Your organisation has no visibility.

Unpredictable costs

Repair bills arrive with no breakdown. Labour and parts are bundled together. Budgeting for device maintenance becomes impossible.

Fragmented vendors

Different vendors for different devices. Different contact numbers. Different quality standards. One point of failure every time a device breaks.

No documentation

Auditors, management committees, and trustees require expense justification. A receipt from a local shop is not documentation. We provide complete repair records.

Most institutions are paying for repairs they cannot verify, from vendors they cannot audit, with no record they can show to anyone. That is the problem Cepheus solves.

02 — What We Offer

A managed repair service built around accountability.

Device Pickup and Delivery

We collect devices from your premises, repair them at our facility, and return them. No staff time wasted travelling to repair shops.

Complete Photo Documentation

Every repair is photographed at each stage — parts removed, parts installed, condition before and after. You receive a full visual record of every job.

Pre-Approved Pricing

We share the diagnosis and exact cost before any work begins. Nothing proceeds without your approval. No surprise invoices.

Consolidated Monthly Invoicing

All repairs in a month consolidated into one clean invoice with full breakdown. Ready for accounts, auditors, and management review.

Priority Response

Institutional bookings get priority pickup slots. We understand that a broken device in a school or office cannot wait three days.

Service Warranty on Every Repair

Every repair carries a warranty period. If the same issue recurs within the warranty window, we fix it at no additional charge.

04 — How It Works

Simple for you. Rigorous for us.

01

You raise a request

WhatsApp us with the device details and issue. We respond within the hour with an estimated price range and pickup slot options.

02

We collect the device

Our team picks up from your premises at the agreed slot. Device is photographed immediately on receipt — condition documented before we touch anything.

03

Diagnosis and approval

We diagnose the issue and share the exact repair cost with parts breakdown. Work begins only after your explicit approval. Nothing proceeds without it.

04

Repair with documentation

Full photographic record at every stage. Parts used are documented — name, type, whether original or compatible. You can see exactly what happened to your device.

05

Return and invoice

Device returned to your premises. Full repair report shared. Monthly consolidated invoice generated for all repairs that month.
